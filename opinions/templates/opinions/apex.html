What this is

DocketDrift collates published opinions from state appellate courts. The goal is to make patterns in published judicial decisions easier to read together — how a judge votes across related cases, how a court treats similar statutes over time. All source material is in the public record.

Our role is collation. We surface case pairs for human review; we don't assert verdicts about consistency or inconsistency.
